Case Studies
DEPATU · PT. Kreativitas Aset Indonesia · 2021–2026
Building brand authority for Indonesia's premier sneaker authentication platform
Nov 2021 – Jun 2026
23K → 129K followers
The Problem
DEPATU had strong product functionality but lacked a clear brand narrative to differentiate it and build deep trust in a trust-sensitive authentication market.
Key Insight
Sneaker owners aren't buying shoes — they're expressing identity. Trust in an authentication platform is about cultural credibility, not just credentials.
Approach
Developed a content-first brand strategy anchored in community and education — positioning DEPATU as a trusted voice in sneaker culture, not just a platform.
Result
Grew Instagram presence from 23K to 129K followers over five years. Contributed to 1–5M+ content impressions and strengthened platform credibility.
Sakken Care · CV. Sakken Group · 2021–2026
Launching a premium shoe care brand in a commoditized market
Dec 2021 – Jun 2026
3 → 29 drop points
The Problem
Entering a market where shoe cleaning was seen as a commodity — differentiated only by price. Needed a strong identity to command premium pricing.
Key Insight
Sneaker enthusiasts aren't looking for the cheapest service — they're looking for one they can trust with their most valued possessions.
Approach
Built the complete brand from zero: positioning, visual identity, tone of voice, packaging, and content strategy focused on trust and emotional value.
Result
Brand supported successful expansion from 3 to 29 drop points across Medan, establishing Sakken Care as a premium, recognizable service brand.

Why visual communication matters in industrial B2B marketing
Most industrial companies have a communication problem — not a product problem.

The technology is world-class. The engineering is proven. But when it comes to talking to customers, partners, and procurement teams online, the message often gets lost in jargon, data sheets, and dense technical copy.

The companies winning attention in mining, manufacturing, and industrial sectors are not the ones with the best product. They are the ones who communicate their value in a way their audience actually understands.
